<p><strong>The Carrageenan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan market is segmented into three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan, each serving distinct applications. Kappa carrageenan forms strong, gel-like textures when combined with potassium ions, making it ideal for dairy products and desserts. Iota carrageenan produces softer gels with calcium ions, suitable for meats and sauces. Lambda carrageenan does not gel but thickens, often used in beverages and dips. Together, these types cater to diverse industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
