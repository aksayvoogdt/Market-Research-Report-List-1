<p><ul><li>Industrial Process</li><li>Oxy-Combustion</li><li>Pre-Combustion</li><li>Post-Combustion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Capture and Sequestration (CCS) market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Industrial Process** captures emissions from industrial activities, like cement and steel production.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Oxy-Combustion** involves burning fossil fuels in pure oxygen, reducing flue gas volume and enabling easier carbon capture.</p><p>3. **Pre-Combustion** captures CO2 before combustion, typically used in integrated gasification combined cycle plants.</p><p>4. **Post-Combustion** captures CO2 from exhaust gases after fuels are burned, applicable to existing power plants and industrial sources. Each method targets emission reduction in distinct operational stages.</p></p>
