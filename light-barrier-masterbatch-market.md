<p><p>The Light Barrier Masterbatch market is categorized into UV and IR segments based on the type of light they target. UV masterbatches are designed to block harmful ultraviolet rays, protecting materials like plastics and coatings from degradation and discoloration. In contrast, IR masterbatches focus on infrared light, often used for applications requiring heat management or thermal control. Both types enhance product performance and longevity, making them valuable in various industries, including packaging, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
