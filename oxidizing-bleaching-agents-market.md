<p><p>The oxidizing bleaching agents market encompasses two main types: oxidizing bleach and reducing bleach. Oxidizing bleach agents, such as hydrogen peroxide and sodium hypochlorite, are used to lighten colors by breaking down chromophores in materials, making them ideal for textiles and paper industries. Conversely, reducing bleaches, like sodium dithionite, eliminate color by converting chromophores into colorless forms, commonly used in dyeing processes. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, driving demand across industries for effective and efficient bleaching solutions.</p></p>
