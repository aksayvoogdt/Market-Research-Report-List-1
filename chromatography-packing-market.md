<p><p>The Chromatography Packing Market includes two primary types: Inorganic Chromatography Packing and Organic Polymer Chromatography Packing. Inorganic packing, often made from silica or alumina, is known for its high mechanical strength, thermal stability, and suitability for high-pressure applications. Organic polymer packing, typically composed of materials like polystyrene or agarose, offers excellent chemical resistance and biocompatibility, making it ideal for biological and pharmaceutical separations. Both types cater to diverse analytical and preparative chromatography needs across industries like pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental testing.</p></p>
