<p><p>The paper cup market features several distinct types, including air pocket insulated, poly-coated, post-consumer fiber, and wax-coated paper cups. Air pocket insulated cups offer superior thermal insulation, keeping beverages hot or cold longer. Poly-coated cups are lined with a plastic coating for moisture resistance, making them suitable for various liquids. Post-consumer fiber cups are made from recycled materials, promoting sustainability. Wax-coated paper cups have a natural wax layer that provides durability and moisture protection, ideal for cold beverages.</p></p>
