<p><p>Three Dimensional Integrated Circuits (3D ICs) enhance performance and reduce power consumption by stacking multiple semiconductor dies. Market types include Through-Silicon Via (TSV), which connects layers vertically using conductive vias, optimizing space and connectivity. Silicon interposers support horizontal connections between chips, facilitating higher bandwidth for complex designs. Through-Glass Via technology uses glass substrates to improve heat dissipation and electrical performance. Other markets encompass emerging solutions such as organic substrates and hybrid bonding, expanding the versatility and application of 3D ICs.</p></p>
