<p><strong>The Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forging market types include closed die, open die, and rolled rings forgings. Closed die forgings involve shaping metal between two dies, allowing for complex shapes and high precision, often used in automotive and aerospace industries. Open die forgings utilize flat or simple shaped dies, ideal for large components and custom shapes, commonly used in various heavy machinery applications. Rolled rings forgings are created by rolling heated billets into rings, suitable for applications requiring lightweight and high-strength components, like piping and turbines.</p></p>
