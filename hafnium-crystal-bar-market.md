<p><ul><li>α Type</li><li>β Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hafnium crystal bars are categorized into two market types: α Type and β Type. α Type hafnium crystal bars are characterized by a hexagonal close-packed (HCP) structure, offering high thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and nuclear applications. β Type hafnium crystal bars have a body-centered cubic (BCC) structure, providing enhanced mechanical strength and ductility, suitable for high-temperature industrial processes. Both types cater to specialized industries, with α Type focusing on stability and β Type emphasizing strength and flexibility.</p></p>
