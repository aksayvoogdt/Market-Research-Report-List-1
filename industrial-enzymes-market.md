<p><p>The industrial enzymes market encompasses various types, including saccharifying enzymes, amylases, proteases, lipases, and others. Proteases facilitate protein degradation, widely used in detergents and food industries. Lipases are involved in fat breakdown, important for dairy and nutritional products. Other enzymes include cellulases and lactases, catering to diverse applications across industries like textiles and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
