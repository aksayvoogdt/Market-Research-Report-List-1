<p><strong>The SFP Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singlemode SFP Module</li><li>Multimode SFP Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SFP module market is divided into two main types: singlemode and multimode SFP modules. Singlemode SFP modules are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a narrow wavelength for minimal signal loss over extended fiber runs. In contrast, multimode SFP modules are ideal for shorter distances, employing a wider core to transmit multiple light modes simultaneously. Both types serve essential roles in networking, connecting switches and routers, with their applications based on specific distance and bandwidth requirements in optical networks.</p></p>
