<p><strong>The Diamond Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroplated Diamond Wire</li><li>Resin Diamond Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond wire market is primarily categorized into two types: electroplated diamond wire and resin diamond wire. Electroplated diamond wire features a layer of diamond particles adhered to a steel core, offering high cutting efficiency and durability, making it ideal for hard materials like granite and marble. In contrast, resin diamond wire incorporates diamond segments bonded with resin, providing a smoother cutting action and lower surface roughness, often used in slicing silicon wafers in the semiconductor industry. Both types cater to specific applications based on cutting needs.</p></p>
