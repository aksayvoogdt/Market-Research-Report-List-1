<p><p>Tetramethrin is a synthetic pyrethroid insecticide widely used for controlling pests in agricultural, residential, and commercial settings. It is effective against a broad range of insects, including mosquitoes, flies, and cockroaches, and is commonly found in household insecticides, mosquito coils, and aerosol sprays. Tetramethrin works by disrupting the nervous system of insects, leading to paralysis and death. Its low toxicity to mammals and rapid knockdown effect make it a popular choice for pest control.</p><p>The Tetramethrin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period.
