<p><p>The noise-canceling earbuds market is divided into two primary types: active and passive noise-canceling earbuds. Active noise-canceling (ANC) earbuds use built-in microphones to detect ambient sounds and produce sound waves that counteract those noises, creating a quieter listening experience. In contrast, passive noise-canceling earbuds rely on physical design and materials to block external sounds, effectively sealing the ear canal. Both types cater to different user preferences and environments, enhancing audio immersion and focus.</p></p>
