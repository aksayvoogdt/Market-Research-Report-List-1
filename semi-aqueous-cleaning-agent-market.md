<p><p>The semi-aqueous cleaning agent market comprises two main types: water-soluble solvent type and water-insoluble solvent type. Water-soluble solvents effectively clean contaminants by dissolving in water, making them suitable for various applications, including electronics and metal cleaning. In contrast, water-insoluble solvents are designed to separate and remove oils and grease without mixing with water, providing effective cleaning for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing efficiency, environmental considerations, and operational requirements.</p></p>
