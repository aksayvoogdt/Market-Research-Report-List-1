<p><strong>The Acoustic Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic biosensors market includes two primary types: Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors. QCM sensors measure mass changes on a sensor surface by monitoring frequency shifts, making them ideal for high-sensitivity biological applications. In contrast, SAW sensors utilize surface acoustic waves to detect changes in mass or mechanical properties, offering advantages like compact size and robustness. Both types are crucial in healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety for real-time biomolecular detection and analysis.</p></p>
