<p><p>Silicone molding materials are specialized for creating durable, flexible components through various methods. Press molding involves placing silicone into heated molds, where heat and pressure shape the material, ideal for large or intricate parts. Conversely, injection molding injects liquid silicone into precisely designed molds under high pressure, allowing for rapid production of complex shapes with tight tolerances. Both techniques cater to different applications in industries like automotive, medical, and consumer goods, enhancing product performance and design versatility.</p></p>
