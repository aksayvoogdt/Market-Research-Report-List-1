<p><p>The fiberglass meshes market is divided into two primary types: interior fiberglass mesh and external fiberglass mesh. Interior fiberglass mesh is used primarily for reinforcing drywall and plaster surfaces, providing strength while ensuring a smooth finish. In contrast, external fiberglass mesh is designed for outdoor applications, such as enhancing the durability of exterior insulation systems and protecting surfaces from weather elements. Both types serve essential purposes in construction and renovation, contributing to structural integrity and longevity of building materials.</p></p>
