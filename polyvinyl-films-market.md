<p><p>The polyvinyl films market comprises various types based on density and structure. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) offers flexibility and toughness, making it ideal for packaging. Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) provides a balance of strength and flexibility, suited for diverse applications like pipe and film production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) features high strength and rigidity, used for containers and industrial applications. Crosslinked Polyethylene (Pex) enhances chemical resistance and durability, commonly utilized in plumbing and electrical insulation. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
