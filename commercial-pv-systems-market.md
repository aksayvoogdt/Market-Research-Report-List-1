<p><p>The commercial photovoltaic (PV) systems market primarily features two types: monocrystalline and polysilicon systems. Monocrystalline systems are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and space optimization but at a higher cost. Polysilicon systems, on the other hand, are produced from multiple silicon crystals, providing a more economical option with slightly lower efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in harnessing solar energy, catering to diverse commercial needs while contributing to sustainability and energy independence.</p></p>
