<p><p>The Commercial and Industrial Energy Storage market encompasses various technologies, including Thermal Energy Storage (TES) and Flywheel Energy Storage. TES stores energy in the form of heat, enabling businesses to utilize stored thermal energy for heating or cooling during peak demand, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs. Flywheel Energy Storage utilizes kinetic energy, storing energy in a spinning rotor, allowing for rapid discharge and grid stabilization. Both systems contribute to energy management, supporting renewable integration and minimizing energy expenses for commercial and industrial sectors.</p></p>
