<p><p>Tungsten nanoparticles are ultrafine particles of tungsten, typically ranging from 1 to 100 nanometers in size. These nanoparticles exhibit unique properties such as high thermal and electrical conductivity, exceptional hardness, and resistance to corrosion, making them valuable in various applications. They are widely used in industries such as electronics, energy, aerospace, and healthcare. In electronics, they are utilized in semiconductors and conductive coatings, while in energy, they play a role in catalysts and energy storage systems.
